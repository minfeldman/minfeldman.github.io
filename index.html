about me

hi! i'm min & i'm a fourth-year student studying psychology, cognitive science, and religion at ohio state.


my coursework & research experiences focus in social cognitive development.


i am interested in social agreement, particularly the roles of collective intentions, obligation, and cooperative and contractual thinking. i hope to research social agreement on the small, interpersonal scale (e.g., friendships, caregiving relationships, promises, trust) and the large scale (e.g., moral rules, social norms, laws, collective action). my interests and methods are informed by developmental psychology, philosophy of social ontology and cognitive science, and sociology.


i'm currently an undergraduate researcher at the thomas lab in the harvard laboratory for developmental studies under dr. ashley thomas and a summer 2025 undergraduate researcher at the boston college cooperation lab under dr. katherine mcauliffe! i'll be joining dr. kurt gray's deepest beliefs lab in the fall and i've previously worked under dr. mark pitt in the language perception lab at ohio state.


in my free time, i love to read and write fiction, watch studio ghibli movies, explore local lakes and swimming holes, take long walks, play piano, and go on bookstore-coffeeshop crawls.

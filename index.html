about me

hi, i'm min! i recently graduated from ohio state, where i studied psychology, cognitive science, and religion as an undergraduate. this fall, i'll be beginning my phd in developmental psychology.


my coursework & research experiences focus in the development of moral and social cognition. i am interested in understanding how young humans think about the moral nature of relationships, with special interest in love, obligations, and social contracts.


in my free time, you can find me reading fiction, swimming, at a ceramics or yoga class, trying new coffeeshops, or on a long walk listening to yacht rock!
